Mega Man

Mega Man X is a human android created by Dr. Thomas Light. Mega Man X is program to have special abilites and can think logically like human. Dr. Light decided to sealed away Mega Man X because he thinks Mega Man X is not ready for his power.

Mega man X is the sucessor of the Dr. Thomas Light's creation. Mega Man X is a human android, that can make his own decisions. Dr. Light fear the among of power from X and decided to sealed X away into a capsule for 30 years. Due to a virus that spread around the world, the robots called Reploids started to attacked humans.
